Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services and process

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but here's a general guideline:

  • Simple course (30 mins): 2-3 weeks
  • Moderate course (1-2 hours): 4-6 weeks
  • Complex program (5+ hours): 8-12 weeks
  • LMS implementation: 4-8 weeks

I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and needs analysis.

How do you ensure accessibility compliance?

Accessibility is built into my design process from the start:

  • WCAG 2.1 AA standards compliance
  • Screen reader compatibility testing
  • Keyboard navigable interfaces
  • Closed captioning for all video
  • Color contrast verification
  • Alternative text for all images

I can also provide VPAT documentation upon request.

What's your pricing structure?

I offer flexible pricing options to suit different needs and budgets:

  • Hourly rate: $95/hour for consulting and development
  • Project-based: Fixed price for defined scope
  • Retainer: Monthly packages for ongoing needs
  • LMS hosting: Monthly subscription options available

Contact me for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you work with subject matter experts?

Absolutely! I have extensive experience collaborating with SMEs to:

  • Extract and organize key information
  • Identify the most critical learning objectives
  • Develop engaging ways to present complex concepts
  • Create realistic scenarios and assessments

I can guide your SMEs through the process to ensure we capture their expertise effectively.

What makes your approach unique?

My methodology combines three key differentiators:

  • Learning Science: Evidence-based strategies that maximize retention and transfer
  • Creative Storytelling: Narrative-driven design that engages learners emotionally
  • Technical Innovation: Cutting-edge interactions without sacrificing usability

This blend results in learning experiences that are both effective and memorable.
